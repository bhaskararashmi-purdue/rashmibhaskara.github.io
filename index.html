Ph.D. Dissertation In progress

Non-Invasive Tumor Microenvironment Characterization and Treatment Response Modeling in NSCLC

Advisor: Dr. Oluwaseyi Oderinde · AdMIRe Lab · Expected 2028

Aim 1
CT → FDG-PET synthesis

Training a 3D conditional optimal transport flow matching model on paired CT/PET from NSCLC-Radiogenomics (n=211) to generate metabolic maps for CT-only patients.

Aim 2
Biological TME state inference

Cross-modal VAE with ViT encoder supervised by RNA-seq-derived immune, hypoxic, and stromal scores to learn a biologically interpretable latent TME state per patient from PET/CT alone.

Aim 3
TME trajectory & survival prediction

Computing per-patient TME trajectory vectors (pre/post chemoRT) and demonstrating improved survival concordance over RECIST and ΔSUVmax baselines.
